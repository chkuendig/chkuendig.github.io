Video Games

I’ve always loved video games. From LAN parties in the early 2000s to working in the industry, gaming has been a constant thread through my life.

In 2014, I moved to Vietnam and joined Gameloft as a game producer in their Hanoi studio. I managed teams of a variety of sizes and spent many nights coordinating releases. It also got me a MobyGames profile.

My full credits there include:

  • UNO & Friends: Update 7 and Update 8 for Google Play and the Amazon Appstore (over 25M downloads for each update)
  • Shark Dash: Relaunch with new game design and monetization strategy for iOS and Google Play (over 5M downloads in the first two months)
  • Ice Age Adventures: Update 1 for Windows Phone and Windows Store

I’ve left the industry since then, but I still love playing and tinkering with games. Nowadays, I either work on ScummVM (porting classic adventure games to run in the browser), or you can find me playing on my PlayStation 4 or Steam Deck.
