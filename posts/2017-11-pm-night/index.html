Product Management Night

November 3, 2017

On November 3rd I had the pleasure to present at the Product Management Night series of the Product Management Festival in Zurich.

This time it was held held at the beautiful NZZ foyer next to the opera house. My presentation focused on how we build deep-tech products a Scandit and how PMs connect technological capabilities and customer value.

I really enjoyed sharing my experiences and connecting with other product managers in my city.
